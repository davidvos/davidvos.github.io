Selected Research

Parameter-Efficient Automation of Data Wrangling Tasks with Prefix-Tuning

David Vos, Till Döhmen, Sebastian Schelter · The Table Representation Learning Workshop @ NeurIPS 2022

Shows that prefix-tuning LLMs can automate data wrangling tasks while requiring only 0.9% of the parameters of full fine-tuning.

Recognised as best paper runner-up.

Revisiting Language Models in Neural News Recommender Systems

Yuyue Zhao, Jin Huang, David Vos, Maarten de Rijke · ECIR 2025

Evaluates how different language models perform as the backbone of neural news recommenders, finding that smaller models can outperform larger ones when properly adapted.

Reliable and Controllable Learned Sparse News Recommendations for Short-History Users

David Vos, Andrew Yates, Maarten de Rijke · Under review

Introduces a learned sparse retrieval (LSR) model that learns sparse representations for users and news, improving retrieval efficiency and short-history recommendation quality.

A Study of Normative Diversity Metrics in News Recommendations

David Vos, Jin Huang, Maarten de Rijke · Under review

Analyzes normative diversity metrics for news recommendations, quantifying their dependence on attribute representation pipelines and editorial context. We also quantify trade-offs between normative diversity and recommendation accuracy.
